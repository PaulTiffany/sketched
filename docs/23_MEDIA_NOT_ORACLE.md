# Media, not oracle (the anti-crutch principle)

*z0 principle, constituted 2026-07-13. Companion to
[08_PERFORMATIVE_INTERPRETATION.md](08_PERFORMATIVE_INTERPRETATION.md),
[14_EULA_MATH_BRIEF.md](14_EULA_MATH_BRIEF.md), and
[16_CONTRIBUTION_PROTOCOL.md](16_CONTRIBUTION_PROTOCOL.md).*

The Lean layer now certifies hundreds of theorems against the Principia
corpus. That creates a specific, foreseeable failure mode, named after
Lanier's *Who Owns the Future*: the stack becomes a **siren server** —
an oracle people lean on instead of think with, concentrating epistemic
authority by treating readers' own differentiation as dispensable. The
tell is the word "efficiency."

The stance of this repository is that the verified stack is **media**:
a deep expression that demands a performer, the way a score does — not
a player piano that replaces one. Each of us must claim our own future;
a certificate can invite that claim, never substitute for it.

This is not a vibe; it is load-bearing architecture, already enforced:

- **Provenance over authority.** Every binding names who transcribed
  what from where, sha-pinned against both corpora. The machine cannot
  fill acceptance fields. Attestation is non-delegable and human.
- **Re-performance over citation.** The reviewer packets say *run
  this*, not *trust us* (`EXPECTED_OUTPUT.md`). Onboarding
  (doc 21) begins with `suite result: CLEAN` on the newcomer's own
  machine — first contact is verification, not belief.
- **The stutter stays.** Unwired helper lemmas, open-anchor lists,
  honesty notes ("the interpretation is NOT certified"), the blank
  genesis signature, dark local traces: the un-quotiented human residue
  is preserved deliberately (the repo-wide non-normalized rule, applied
  to the human layer). Rum pa pum pum: the offering is played at
  capacity, imperfection included, and the imperfection is part of the
  value. Polishing it away is how siren servers are born.
- **The math itself warns against its own idolization.** The Moloch
  hedge (SRMFHelix.lean): perpetual ascent on a floored potential is a
  theorem-level impossibility; approach-without-arrival (`with_and_was`)
  is the honest relation between any finite performance and its limit.

Practical rule for anything public-facing (docs, lessons, Matt/Ellie
renders, streams): frame the Lean layer as an **invitation to
perform** — here is the score, here is the instrument, here is how to
check us — never as an answer machine. If a presentation would leave
its audience *less* inclined to differentiate for themselves, it
violates this document.
